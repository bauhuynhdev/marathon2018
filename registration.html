RULES & REGULATIONS
- There will be no fee refund for a participant for any reason whatsoever.
- A participant is strictly not allowed to transfer his or her race entry and BIB number to another party.
- Any transfer case will be considered as invalid registration.
- Invalid registration will not be entitled for the benefits of participant and will not be considered for the race result.
- Free entrances will not be considered for the final event prizes.
- No changes of race categories are allowed after To be Updated.
- If the participant provides any information that is untrue, inaccurate, not current or incomplete, the organizer may suspend or terminate the participant’s registration.
- The decision of the organizer is the final decision.
